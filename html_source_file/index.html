About Me

I am an M.S. student at KAIST Graduate School of AI, advised by Prof. Minhyuk Sung. I received my B.S. degree in Computer Science at KAIST in Aug. 2023.

My research interests revolve around enabling AI to understand and generate the complex structure of the physical 3D world. In particular, my current research area includes, but is not limited to, 2D/3D Generative Models, Diffusion Models, and Multimodal Understanding.

For details, refer to my Curriculum Vitae (CV).
