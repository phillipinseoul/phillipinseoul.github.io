About Me

I am Phillip (Yuseung) Lee, an M.S. student at KAIST Graduate School of AI, advised by Prof. Minhyuk Sung. I received my B.S. degree in Computer Science at KAIST in Aug. 2023.

My research interests revolve around enhancing the spatial control and understanding of large foundation models. Recently, I have worked on improving the spatial control of diffusion models and am also interested in advancing the 3D spatial awareness of multimodal LLMs and vision-language models.

For details, refer to my Curriculum Vitae (CV).
